What is WordPicker?

Find words from letters and patterns for all popular word games such as Scrabble, Wordscraper, Lexulous, Words With Friends, Draw Something and 4 Pics 1 Word. It can even help with Boggle-like grid games, word ladder & crosswords too. Use the advanced pattern matching facilities to help find words to fit around other words:

? matches a blank
$ matches the beginning or end of a word
[abc] matches one letter from a specific set
